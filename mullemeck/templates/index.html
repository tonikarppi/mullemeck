To view all the information about single builds you can click on the Build View header. Alternetively you can write /build_view/x in the URL bar, where x is the number of the build you want to view. To look through the history of builds, multiple builds can be viewed at once on the build list page. To view this you can click on the Build List header or write /build_list/1 in the URL bar to get to the first page of the list.
